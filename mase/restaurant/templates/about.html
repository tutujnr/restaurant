About Us

Based in Kisumu, Maseno Streets. It is a family owned which focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from Kalenjin, Swahili, and African culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

The restaurant is owned by two Kenyan brothers, Aron and Evans, who unite together to make sure the restaurant runs smoothly.

To craft the menu, Clara relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in Kenya.

Aron does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Kenyan to incorporate additional cuisines from the Nyanza region.
